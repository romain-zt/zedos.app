## How usage works (accordion)

- Your plan includes enough AI for **typical founder workflows** (clarify, generate, iterate).
- **Express run:** usually under 20% of one Builder month.
- **Full standard PRD:** about one “PRD generation” unit plus clarify steps.
- Need more? **One-time top-up** — not a surprise subscription.
- First PRD journey: we don’t cut you off mid-session once (see FAQ).

## FAQ

**Why not unlimited like ChatGPT?**  
ChatGPT forgets. Zedos keeps **decisions, versions, and Cursor handoff** in one place.

**What’s express?**  
Minimum questions → 12-section shareable livrable, labeled *express — to be deepened*.

**Can I stay on Free?**  
Yes — best for trying express and one share. Upgrade when you export to Cursor or run multiple projects.

**EU / US billing?**  
Stripe — FR/EU + US. VAT shown at checkout for digital services.

**Cancel anytime?**  
Yes. Monthly plans — no annual lock-in on Builder founding offer.
