# LocalMkt — Standard PRD (exemple galerie)

> Fictif — démo galerie Zedos.

## vision

Urban households get weekly baskets from suburban producers, with transparent pricing and &lt; 24h farm-to-door in metro areas.

## target_users

**Buyer:** Dual-income household, 30–45, cares about provenance, not price-sensitive on premium tier.  
**Seller:** Small producer, 1–5 ha, already at farmers market, needs demand forecast.

## core_features

1. Producer catalog + harvest calendar  
2. Basket builder (consumer)  
3. Route batching for delivery partner  

## user_journeys

Consumer subscribes Tuesday basket → producer confirms harvest Thursday → delivery Saturday AM.

## technical

Mobile-first PWA, partner API for last-mile, cold-chain checklist PDF.

## success_metrics

- 40% repeat order rate week 4  
- Producer utilization &gt; 70% of listed SKUs  

## out_of_scope

National shipping, B2B restaurant supply.

## risks

Cold chain failure — insurance and SLA with delivery partner TBD.
